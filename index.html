Conway's Game of Life

4 main principles to follow:
Underpopulation:
- If a cell has < 2 living neighbors, it dies due to loneliness
Survival:
- Living cell with 2 or 3 living neighbors survives
Overpopulation:
- If a living cell has > 3 living neighbors, it dies due to overpopulation
Reproduction:
- Dead cell with exactly 3 living neighbors becomes alive through reproduction

Made using the p5.js library in Javascript.
The preloaded pattern is known as Gosper glider gun:
- Emits a new glider every 30 generations
- Significant in the study of cellular automata
The game also allow you to draw your own patterns.
